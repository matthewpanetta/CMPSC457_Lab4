Instructions

In this lab I needed to:
  • Draw a 3D object
  • Use at least six sub-objects
  • Modify the object's animation

My Implementation

  • I made a robot. The robot starts walking if 'a' is pressed.
  • The robot is made up of 2 wire spheres (eyes), 2 wire cubes (teeth), and 6 solid cubes. (everything else)
  • When 'a' is pressed, the robot will move its arms and legs to simulate walking.
  • This utilizes a rotate variable. Once the variable gets to a certain value, it reverses direction.
  • The robot will stay in place during the animation.
  • The robot can be moved and rotated when not in animation mode (like the car)
